About Obalola Lumière Films

Welcome to the world of Obalola Lumière Films — where visual storytelling is elevated to an artform.

Born from the rhythm of Lagos and rooted in the soul of Kogi, Obalola's eye for motion captures beauty in its purest form. With a signature style that blends complexity with calmness, every frame is crafted with meaning, music, and emotion.

Whether directing music videos, crafting visuals for emerging artists, or building cinematic worlds on a budget — Obalola Lumière Films brings clarity, creativity, and cultural pride to the forefront.

Our mission is simple: to empower artists and brands with videos that move hearts and break limits.

In the silent dance of light and shadow, I weave stories only the heart can hear — where complexity finds calm, and every frame whispers a dream.
— Abdulmalik Idris Bala

My Journey

2023: Started exploring music video directing with basic equipment and passion.
2024: Developed my unique style combining calmness with complexity, and founded Obalola Lumière Films.
2025: Built a portfolio, launched my website, and began working with emerging artists across Nigeria.
